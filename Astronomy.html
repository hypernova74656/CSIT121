Gamma Ray Bursts

Gamma ray bursts can occur during a supernova. Energetic jets are emitted by the dieing star. I learned about gamma ray bursts during a conference I attended while I was working on a degree in physics. I decided to do research on gamma ray bursts when I went to graduate school.
