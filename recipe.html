Bowl of Cereal with Fruit

Refreshing Summer Dinner!

Ingredients:
  • Cereal
  • Milk
  • Fruit
How to prepare:
  1. Obtain a bowl
  2. Pour desired amount of cereal into the bowl
  3. Pour desired amount of milk into the bowl
  4. Cut up the desired amount of fruit
  5. Add fruit to the bowl

A bowl of cereal with fruit is a great dinner during the summer when it is too hot to use the stove.
